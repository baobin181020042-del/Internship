## AWS: Enterprise Cloud Architectures and Industry Application featuring Cloud Kinetics & Renova Cloud

- **Time:** Morning, July 04, 2026
- **Location:** Hybrid event held on floor 26 of Bitexco Financial Tower
- **Participants:** The event also included students from Swinburne Vietnam, including the Can Tho campus.
- **Role:** Online attendee

## Event Objectives

- Help students learn about cloud computing and enterprise application architecture.
- Share practical experience, career orientation, soft skills, and effective AI usage.
- Help students understand market expectations in cloud, data, and AI.

## Speakers

- Nguyen Tran Minh Duy - Industry Liaison Officer at Swinburne Vietnam.
- Nguyen Gia Hung - Head of Solution Architect at AWS Vietnam, Founder of First Cloud AI Journey.
- Banh Cam Vinh - Data Engineer at Renova Cloud, AWS Community Builder.
- Nhu Tran - Account Manager at AWS Vietnam.
- Khang Nguyen - Solution Architect at Cloud Kinetics, Swinburne alumnus.

## Key Highlights

### Cloud Computing job market

Cloud demand in Vietnam is growing quickly, but the market is also more demanding. Freshers and interns may need cloud native, Kubernetes, AI developer mindset, and industry knowledge.

### Industry-based thinking

Engineers should not only know their role but also understand industry context such as finance, retail, or operations to create real business value.

### Data Platform architecture

The session explained differences between academic data and real business data. Tools may change, but architecture thinking around ingestion, processing, storage, governance, catalog, and analytics remains important.

### Communication and visibility

Students should build networks, practice small talk, and create visibility instead of relying only on mass CV submission.

### Mindset in the AI era

AI amplifies existing capability. If the foundation is weak, AI output can be wrong and hard to validate. For freshers, learning attitude remains extremely important.

## What I Learned

- Career growth can be viewed through Capability x Visibility x Consistency.
- Architecture thinking is more important than memorizing a single tool.
- Every technical decision has trade-offs in cost, risk, time, and operations.

## Application to My Work

- Improve my profile/CV through side projects, technical blogs, public code, and lifelong learning.
- Use AI to review code, improve architecture, and learn more deeply, not just to complete assignments quickly.
- Increase visibility by joining communities, hackathons, and networking with seniors.

## Event Experience

This event gave me a broader view of the cloud career path. I realized that technical skill is only one part; business understanding, communication, asking questions, and visibility are also important. The community's Forward spirit motivated me to keep learning and share back with others.
